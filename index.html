About Us

UMUSADA EVENTS is an online platform. We focus on organizing, creating, and sharing different types of volunteer and fundraising events across the country. These volunteer and fundraising events vary from those which can be executed fully online, on-site, or both. Online volunteer events are those events with a goal of raising funds to help those in need of support. On-site volunteer events can vary from organizing blood donations, cleaning places (Umuganda), Providing food and shelter, and many other events with a goal of helping those in need.
